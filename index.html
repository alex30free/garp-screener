How the automation works
You do

Upload your new Börsdata export. Everything else is automatic. No buttons to click, no renaming files.

After each run, the qualifying tickers are saved in your browser's local storage. Next quarter, they're loaded automatically as the "previous" list — giving you HOLD and SELL signals without any extra steps.
